 It is 1944, and Sam and Susy Hendrix live in a basement apartment in Greenwich Village. Susy is blind, but determined to cope with life. Sam loves her and encourages her efforts to be independent. While on a business trip to Canada, a strange woman put a doll into his duffel bag to get it over the border. She comes to the apartment to claim it a day later, explaining that she must have mixed up their bags. Sam can't find it and does not realize that it is very valuable. Later, two petty criminals and Roat, a career con man, turn up at the apartment looking for the doll. Sam is not home, and Susy eventually realizes that they are not who they claim to be. A deadly game of cat and mouse ensues until Susy equalizes things by putting out all the lights. This is a classic thriller with a nearly unbearable final scene.
