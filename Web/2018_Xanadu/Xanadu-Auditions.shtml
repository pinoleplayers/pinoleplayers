XANADU follows the journey of a magical and beautiful Greek muse, Kira, who descends from the heavens of Mount Olympus to Venice Beach, California in 1980! Her quest is to inspire a struggling artist, Sonny, to achieve the greatest artistic creation of all time - the first ROLLER DISCO! (Hey, it's 1980!) But, when Kira falls into forbidden love with the mortal, her jealous sisters take advantage of the situation, and chaos abounds.

This campy, loving homage to the 80s is a moving, electrifying tale of endless fun that will keep audiences in stitches, while the original legendary, chart-topping tunes will lift them out of their seats. You'll want to keep the music in your head and XANADU in your heart... forever.

Audition Process

  • Please prepare two songs:
    • An Up-Tempo Modern Broadway or Pop Song
    • A Modern Broadway or Pop Ballad
      • Sing 16-32 Bars - up to one minute - of each
      • NO Stephen Sondheim, Leonard Bernstein or Jason Robert Brown
      • Bring sheet music IN YOUR KEY
      • An accompanist will be provided.
      • MP3 backing tracks are discouraged
      • No a capella
  • A short dance audition will be included; dress for movement
  • Cold readings from the script, time permitting.
    • See the Character Descriptions Below
  • ALL cast members will roller skate in the show.
    • Current skill level is not important; those cast will be trained.

Auditions are by appointment.

Appointments for 30 minute audition slots will be assigned in the order they are received, beginning each day at the times above.

CHARACTERS
NameGenderAgeVocal
Range		Description
Clio/KiraF20-30Soprano/
Mezzo		 Muse of History, and leader of the Muses. She comes to Earth as Kira to help the mortal, Sonny Malone, in hopes of fulfilling Zeus' prophecy, "that one day she will inspire a creation so wondrous that she will be granted the gift of Xanadu." She falls in love with Sonny, an act forbidden by the gods, and, in turn, endures the wrath of her father, Zeus. The actor playing Clio/Kira spends almost the entire show in roller skates. Soprano/Mezzo with strong comedic timing.
Sonny MaloneM25-35Tenor/
Baritone		 Handsome and very "1980s." He is a struggling artist. When we first meet him, he is working on a mural of the Nine Muses. But his dream is to open "an apex of all the arts" - a roller disco. He falls in love with Clio, disguised as Kira, as she attempts to inspire his art. Tenor/Baritone, with good comic timing and leading man looks. Must move well in roller skates.
Danny Maguire/
Zeus		M45+Baritone This very professional, very successful businessman is a former clarinet player, who gave up his dreams to become a real estate mogul. He was once in love with a Southern belle named Kitty (Clio in disguise), who left him when he refused to open the Xanadu, a theater he had built for her. Baritone with strong acting chops.
MelpomeneF35+Mezzo/
Alto Belter		 Muse of Tragedy. As the eldest of the Muses, she resents that Zeus made Clio - and not her - leader of the Muses. She takes revenge on Clio with a curse to make her fall in love with the mortal Sonny, and so, be condemned to eternal damnation in the netherworld. Strong Mezzo/Alto Belter.
CalliopeF35+Mezzo Belter Muse of Epics. Quirky sidekick to Melpomene. Strong Mezzo Belter.
Ensemble3M,3F18+Various The ensemble must be quadruple threats, as they portray every other character in this show. They must be strong dancers, strong singers, easily handling harmonies, strong actors, able to play multiple small but EXTREMELY important character roles, and be able to roller skate (or willing to learn). All in all, a very busy ensemble.
