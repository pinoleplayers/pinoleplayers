One fateful night, Brad Majors and his fiancee, Janet Weiss - a wholesome, utterly normal couple in love - innocently set out to visit an old professor. A thunderstorm and a flat tire force them to seek help at the castle of Dr Frank'N'Furter, a mad scientist with a manic genius and an insatiable libido. Brad, Janet and Frank'N'Furter's cohorts are swept up into the scientist's latest experiment. The night's misadventures will cause Brad and Janet to question everything they've known about themselves, each other, love and lust.

Since OUTSIDE PROPS are NOT ALLOWED, you can still participate by PURCHASING a PARTICIPATION KIT when you order tickets or at the door. Each kit contains all the props for PCP's ROCKY HORROR SHOW:

  • Balloon (for you to inflate)
  • Newpaper
  • Noise Maker
  • Toilet Paper
  • Toast (bagged up tight!)
  • Glo Stick(a lighter app on your phone is OK)
  • Playing Cards
  • Participation Script (explaining when to use each prop and some of the common TALK BACK lines)
