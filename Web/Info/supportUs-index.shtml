YOU CAN SUPPORT US IN MANY WAYS

SEE A SHOW

After all, it's what we do. The PINOLE COMMUNITY PLAYERS exists because of our audiences, the people who pay to see our productions, who come to our Playhouse to be entertained.

Ticket revenue doesn't cover the cost of a show, but full houses help.

TELL YOUR FRIENDS

Word of mouth is a very effective way to fill our seats. If you enjoyed a show, maybe some friends or family members will. How about arranging a night out at the theatre with friends.

If your group is 15 or more, you can save with our Group Rate.

For groups of 15 or more, tickets are $18 each for musicals, $13 each for non-musicals.
Please leave your name and phone number on the Box Office Line at 510-724-9844 and our Ticket Coordinator will return your call to take your group order.

VOLUNTEER

PCP exists because of the work of hundreds of people.

They don't do it for money - there isn't any left after we finance our productions. They do it for love - most for love of theatre, some for love of community, some for love of family.

They come from every walk of life - adults, teenagers, even children. They become carpenters, seamstresses, actors, musicians, janitors, stage hands, technicians, stage managers, directors, concessionaires, ticket sellers, ushers, all of the roles required to run a theatre company, produce a show, present a show.

Perhaps you'd like to be one of those amazing people...
