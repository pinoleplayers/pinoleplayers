THE 2016 RENOVATION OF THE PLAYHOUSE
 
Part of the preparations for celebrating PCP's thirtieth year was a renovation of the Pinole Community Playhouse. Permanent seating, new carpeting and freshened paint warmed the theater and invigorated the lobby to begin our second 30 years. Planning and fund-raising took place over years. The final phase of refreshing the interior decorations took weeks of work by a small group of talented volunteers.
Come to a show and enjoy our beautiful, refreshed space!
