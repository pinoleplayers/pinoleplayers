Singin' in the Rain is the story of the making of the first Hollywood musical. A silent-screen swashbuckler finds love while trying to adjust to the coming of sound. It features all the style and spectacle of a golden age and a succession of unforgettable songs, including Good Morning, Make 'em Laugh,Moses Supposes, and, of course, Singin' in the Rain.
