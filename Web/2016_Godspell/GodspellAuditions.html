 Prepare ye for this timeless tale of friendship, loyalty and love based on the Gospel According to St. Matthew.

This musical is a series of parables, mostly based on the Gospel of Matthew, though three appear only in the Gospel of Luke. The parables are interspersed with songs with lyrics from traditional hymns set to modern music, with the passion of Christ treated briefly near the end of the performance.
