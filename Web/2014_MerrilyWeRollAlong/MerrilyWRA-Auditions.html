ABOUT THE SHOW: Merrily We Roll Along is probably best remembered as one of Stephen Sondheim's glorious flops. Its 16-performance run on Broadway was only underperformed by Anyone Can Whistle's nine. Despite this, it's remembered for its fantastic, energetic score - the closest Sondheim has come in style (along with Follies) to musicals of the Golden Age. From 1994 onward, Merrily has been rewritten in book and score several times, culminating in the 2013 West End premiere of the show that sold out its extended run and was hailed a triumph by the English press. That's the version we'll be putting on.
