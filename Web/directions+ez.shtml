Pinole Community Playhouse
601 Tennent Ave
Pinole, CA 94564

From Martinez/Concord
  • Take Hwy-4 West to I-80 West
  • Then follow the From Vallejo Directions below.
From Vallejo
  • Take I-80 West to Pinole Valley Road Exit
  • Turn Right on Pinole Valley Road
  • At second light, go straight on Tennent Ave.
  • Go through one more light (San Pablo Ave.)
  • Playhouse is on the Right next to the baseball field
From Richmond/Berkeley/Oakland /San Francisco
  • Take I-80 East to Pinole Valley Road Exit
  • Turn Left on Pinole Valley Road
  • At third light, go straight on Tennent Ave.
  • Go through one more light (San Pablo Ave.)
  • Playhouse is on the Right next to the baseball field
From Marin
  • Take I-580 East across the Richmond-San Rafael Bridge
  • Past the Toll Plaza, take Exit 7 I-80 East/Richmond Pkwy/Point Richmond
  • Merge onto E Standard Ave.
  • Continue onto Castro St.
  • Continue onto the Richmond Pkwy
  • Turn Left on San Pablo Ave.
  • Turn Left on Tennent Ave.
  • Playhouse is on the Right next to the baseball field
