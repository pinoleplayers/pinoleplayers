This charming new musical invites you to a wedding day you will never forget, where anything that can go wrong does and where love pops up in mysterious places. The bride is Jewish. The groom is Catholic. Her mother is a force of nature. His mother is a tempest in a cocktail shaker. When the bride's ex-boyfriend crashes the party, the perfect wedding unravels faster than you can whistle "Oh Promise Me!"

Audition Process

  • Warm Reading From Script
    • See the Character Descriptions Below
    • The excerpts from the script (the "Sides") to be read will be provided in your appointment confirmation email
      • can be obtained via the links below
    • Print out the Sides for the character(s) you want to read in the audition. Bring them to the audition.
  • One minute monologue, if you desire. not required
  • Prepare a 1-2 minute song from a contemporary musical
    • Bring sheet music IN YOUR KEY. An accompanist will be provided
    • No tapes, CDs or electronic media
    • A capella is strongly discouraged
  • Dance audition at callbacks

Auditions are by appointment.

Audition appointments will be assigned in the order they are received, beginning at 7:00 PM each evening.

If you have time constraints, include them and we will attempt to accommodate you.
