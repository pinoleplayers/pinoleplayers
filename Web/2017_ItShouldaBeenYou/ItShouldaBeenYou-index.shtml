This charming new musical invites you to a wedding day you will never forget, where anything that can go wrong does and where love pops up in mysterious places. The bride is Jewish. The groom is Catholic. Her mother is a force of nature. His mother is a tempest in a cocktail shaker. When the bride's ex-boyfriend crashes the party, the perfect wedding unravels faster than you can whistle "Oh Promise Me!"

Deathtrap is a thriller in two-acts, with dark comic undertones.

more

Once the toast of Broadway, Sidney Bruhl is a playwright with a bad case of writer's block. Clifford Bradshaw, a student attending Sidney's writing seminars, shares the draft of DEATHTRAP, a play he has written. Sidney is sure it will be a smash and gets a killer idea! Murder Clifford and pass the play off as his own.

Twists and turns abound in this clever and funny Broadway hit, a 1978 Tony nominee for Best Play.
