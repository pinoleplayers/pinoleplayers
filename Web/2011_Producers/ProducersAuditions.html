Auditions
This zany Mel Brooks musical is not for the faint of heart. Skewering lots of show biz sacred cows, laced with politically incorrect humor, this show has six principal roles (5 men, 1 woman) and a large ensemble who get to present hits like "Springtime For Hitler" and "Keep It Gay." With 29 designated roles to cover, the ensemble will be more than busy. Chorus girls, cops and storm troopers all join in the fun, not to mention pigeons who "Heil Hitler!" It's all about silly fun and crazy schemes that take the audience on a comedy journey.
