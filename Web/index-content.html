Directed by Janet O’Brien

Music by David Shire
Lyrics by Richard Maltby, Jr.
Book by Sybille Pearson

Three couples, one just starting out, one having trouble conceiving, one looking forward to an empty nest, experience the emotional stresses and triumphs, the desperate lows and the comic highs, that accompany the anticipation and arrival of a BABY.
