COMING SOON

Produced By
The Pinole Community Players

A Broadway Musical
based on the 1952 movie.

Set in Hollywood in the waning days of the silent screen ear, focusing on romantic lead Don Lockwood, sidekick, Cosmo Brown, aspiring actress Kathy Selden, and leading lady, Lina Lamont, whose less-than-dulcet vocal tones make her an unlikely candidate for stardom in the talking pictures.

Directed and Choreographed by
Justin Cole and
Anjee Norgaard
Music Direction: Sierra Dee
Set Design: Fernando Ochoa

COMING IN APRIL

Produced By
West County Musicals
formerly Pinole Youth Musicals

Book by Guy Bolton, P.G. Wodehouse, Howard Lindsay and Russell Crouse

Music and Lyrics by COLE PORTER

A Broadway Musical about the madcap antics aboard an ocean liner bound from New York to London.
Billy Crocker is a stowaway in love with heiress Hope Harcourt, who is engaged to Lord Evelyn Oakleigh. Nightclub singer Reno Sweeney and Public Enemy #13 Moonface Martin aid Billy in his quest to win Hope. The musical introduced such songs as Anything Goes, You're the Top, and I Get a Kick Out of You.

Directed by Dave Clark
Musical Direction: Dean Starnes
Choreography: Anjee Norgaard
Set Design: Fernando Ochoa
