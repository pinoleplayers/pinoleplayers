Everything you have ever secretly thought about dating, romance, marriage, lovers, husbands, wives and in-laws, but were afraid to admit.

I Love You, You're Perfect, Now Change is written for anyone who has ever been in a relationship, wanted to be in a relationship, thought about being in a relationship or has seen a television program or movie about a relationship. The original Off-Broadway Production ran for over 4500 performances - one of the longest running productions in New York theatre history. If Saturday Night Live did a sketch comedy show about relationships set to music... it would look a lot like I Love You, You're Perfect, Now Change.
