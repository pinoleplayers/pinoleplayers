January 20 - February 11, 2017
Presented though special arrangement with Samuel French, Inc.
Book by
Fred Ebb and Bob Fosse
Music by John Kander
Lyrics by Fred Ebb
Directed by Janet H.O'Brien

Broadway's longest-running American musical, CHICAGO is a dazzling and satirical look at fame, justice and the media machine. Set in the 1920s, CHICAGO follows Roxie Hart, a wannabe vaudevillian who murders her lover and is arrested. In jail, Roxie meets her hero, the famed double murderess Velma Kelly. When both acquire the same lawyer, superstar Billy Flynn, tensions come to a head as each lady vies for the "spotlight!"

June 2 - 17, 2017

Presented by special arrangements with Dramatists Play Service, Inc.
Written by Ira Levin
Directed by G. A. Klein

Once the toast of Broadway, Sidney Bruhl is a playwright with a bad case of writer's block. Clifford Bradshaw, a student attending Sidney's writing seminars, shares the draft of a play he has written, DEATHTRAP. Sidney is sure it will be a smash and gets a killer idea! Murder Clifford and pass the play off as his own. Plot twists abound as fatal fiction becomes fatal fact in this clever, often funny and suspenseful Broadway smash.

October 20 - November 11, 2017
Presented through special arrangement with Music Theatre International
Music by Barbara Anselmi
Book & Lyrics by Brian Hargrove
Directed by Buzz Halsing

The charming new musical, IT SHOULDA BEEN YOU, invites you to a wedding day you will never forget, where anything that can go wrong does and where love pops up in mysterious places. The bride is Jewish. The groom is Catholic. Her mother is a force of nature. His mother is a tempest in a cocktail shaker. When the bride's ex-boyfriend crashes the party, the perfect wedding unravels faster than you can whistle "Oh Promise Me!"
